Beat the “too lazy to tidy” habit. Track steps and calories while you clean—your space, easier.

Support Privacy

How it works

Tap Start, put your phone in your pocket, and move around while you tidy. When you’re done, tap End to see your steps and estimated calories. Set your weight for better estimates, get weekly reminders, and share sessions. Premium adds flexible reminders (e.g. every 3 or 5 days), weekly and monthly stats, charts up to a year, and a home screen widget. 3-day free trial.

Support

Need help with TidyStep?

  • How to use: Open the app → Start → put your phone in your pocket and tidy → End to see steps and calories.
  • Premium: Unlock statistics, flexible reminders, and the home screen widget via in-app subscription (3-day free trial). Restore purchases in Settings if you already subscribed.
  • Data: All data is stored on your device. We don’t collect personal data or require an account.

For feedback or issues, contact us via the app’s support channel or your usual store listing.
